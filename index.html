Meet Your Lending Partner

I'm Leon, and I've been helping realtors close deals since 2002.

The most common question I get from realtors is simple: "How fast can you close?" My answer is always the same — if your client signs their application, provides income and asset documentation, and has an executed contract, I can generate a loan commitment within 24 business hours.

When title is ready and the appraisal is waived, I can close in as little as a week. For standard deals, we're typically done in under 30 days.

No one can guarantee a closing date — but I clear conditions in 24 business hours. That's my promise to you and your clients.
